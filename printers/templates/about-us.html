Who we are

For over 25 years, we are helping customers to celebrate their special moments by delivering fresh flowers and fabulous gifts. Ferns N Petals was established in 1994 and flower bouquet delivery was the first and only thing that the company was into. From just 1 flower shop in Delhi during 1994, Ferns N Petals is now the largest florist chain with more than 320 stores (and still counting) across all the major cities and towns of India.

Today, Ferns N Petals Group encompasses of printunlock Retail & Franchising, printunlock E-commerce- India, UAE, Singapore & Qatar, printunlock Weddings & Events, Floral Touch- India & UAE, printunlock Gardens, Handicrafts, The Flagship Store by printunlock, WDH (Wedding Design Hub), printunlock Cakes 'N' More and printunlock Media. The brand is one stop shop for all gifting needs, be it buying flowers, cakes, plants, personalized gifts, decoration services, or experiences, not only in India but across the globe.
